# Case — Modern Mechanisms for Educational Continuity for Hospitalized Learners

Conceptual framework paper on hospital pedagogy as **inclusive education mechanism**. 2026.

## Core claim
Hospital pedagogy has evolved from auxiliary service to **critical mechanism of inclusive education** for learners with health-related attendance disruption.

## Three delivery modes (load-bearing for our design)
1. **Bedside teaching** — 1:1 at the patient's room.
2. **Asynchronous micro-learning** — self-paced, short-form digital content.
3. **Moderated synchronous connection to home classroom** — video link to peers.

> Our pilot = a **novel 4th mode**: synchronous in-person large-group event at a designated venue (not bedside, not remote). Framework does not cover this.

## Five core mechanisms
1. Shared governance across stakeholders (hospital, school, family).
2. Individualized continuity plans.
3. Hybrid delivery models.
4. Safeguarding & data stewardship.
5. Reintegration pathways.

## Constraints the framework identifies
- Unpredictable clinical routines.
- **Fatigue**.
- **Infection control requirements**.
- **Cognitive side effects of treatment**.
- Psychosocial stress.

- No session-duration or pacing recommendations.
- No infection protocols for group gatherings.

- Purely conceptual — no implementation case studies.
